Walkthrough: How to run Cufflinks

by Niema Moshiri

  • Once the TopHat app completes, you can run the Cufflinks app on the TopHat output. Enter the “Apps” tab at the top of the BaseSpace page, and click on the “Cufflinks Assembly & DE” app.


  • Select version 1.1.0 on the top-right of the page, and click “Launch”.


  • Next to “Save Results To,” click “Select Project(s)”.


  • Select the “RNA-seq Methods Results” project you created in a previous step, and click “Confirm.”


  • Under “Control Group,” in the box next to “Group Label,” enter “UHRR”.


  • Also under “Control Group,” next to “TopHat Alignment App Result(s),” click “Select App Result(s):”

  • Select “mRNA-UHRR-C1” and “mRNA-UHRR-C2” and click “Confirm”.


  • Under “Comparison Group,” in the box next to “Group Label,” enter “Brain”.


  • Also under “Comparison Group,” next to “TopHat Alignment App Result(s),” click “Select App Result(s):”.


  • Select “mRNA-Brain-C4” and “mRNA-Brain-C6”, and click “Confirm”.


  • Click “Continue” to start running the application.


    • The Cufflinks application (with CuffDiff included) will take approximately two hours to finish running.
