Walkthrough: How to perform hierarchical clustering with GenePattern

by Niema Moshiri

  • Go to the following link and create a GenePattern account using the “Click to Register” button. Then sign in using your login information. http://genepattern.broadinstitute.org/gp/pages/login.jsf


  • Visit the following link (the “HierarchicalClustering” module): http://genepattern.broadinstitute.org/gp/pages/index.jsf?lsid=HierarchicalClustering


  • Next to “input filename*”, click “Upload File…” and choose “human_mouse.gct” (you can download this file at this link.


  • Click the “Run” button to the right to queue the job (should take less than a minute to finish, typically seconds).


  • When the job finishes (you can click on the link “Hierarchical Clustering” and click “Job Status” on the pop-out left-hand menu to see the job status), two output files will be created: “human_mouse.atr” and “human_mouse.cdt”. Download both files by clicking on each and clicking “Save File” in the left-hand menu that pops out.



  • Visit the following link (the “HierarchicalClusteringViewer” module): http://genepattern.broadinstitute.org/gp/pages/index.jsf?lsid=HierarchicalClusteringViewer. Next to “cdt file*”, click “Upload File…” and select “human_mouse.cdt”


  • Next to “atr file”, click “Upload File…” and select “human_mouse.atr”


  • Click the “Run” button at the bottom-right of the page to queue the job


  • When the job finishes, click “Open Visualizer” if the visualizer does not open automatically.
