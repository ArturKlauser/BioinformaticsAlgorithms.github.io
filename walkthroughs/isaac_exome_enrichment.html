Using Isaac to analyze genome sequencing data

by Niema Moshiri

  • Open the “Apps” tab in BaseSpace and select “Enrichment”.

  • Select version 2.1.1 and click “Launch” in the upper-right portion of the page.

  • Next to “Save results to:”, click “Select Project(s):”, select the project named “WGS vs. WES Results” you created in the WGS analysis portion, and click “Confirm”.

  • Next to “Sample:”, click “Select Sample(s):”, select “NA12878-NRC-Exome,” and click “Confirm”.

  • Make sure that, for “Targeted Regions,” “Nextera Rapid Capture Exome v1.2” is selected, and click “Continue” to begin the execution of the app. The Nextera Rapid Capture Exome v1.2 is simply the lab kit used to extract the exomic DNA. This app takes approximately two hours to finish running. As in our WGS analysis, the app automatically performs both sequence alignment and variant calling, so a VCF file will automatically be output.
