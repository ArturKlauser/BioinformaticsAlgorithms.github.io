Walkthrough: How to run the STAR+DESeq workflow

by Niema Moshiri

  • Click on the “RNA Express” app, select version 1.0.0 on the top-right of the page, and click “Launch”.


  • Next to “Save Results To,” click “Select Project(s).”


  • Click “New…,”


  • Name the project “RNA-seq Methods Results,” and click “Create.”


  • Click “Confirm" again with the “RNA-seq Methods Results” folder selected.


  • Under “Control Group,” first change the label to “UHRR".


  • Then click “Select Samples”.


  • Choose the two UHRR samples (mRNA-UHRR-C1 and mRNA-UHRR-C1), and click “Confirm.”


  • For the “Comparison Group,” first change the label to “Brain,”


  • Click “Select Sample(s)".


  • Choose the two brain samples (mRNA-Brain-C4 and mRNA-Brain-C6), and click “Confirm.”


  • Then, click the “Continue” button to the right.
