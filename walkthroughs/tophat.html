Walkthrough: How to run TopHat

by Niema Moshiri

  • Open the “Apps” tab, and click on the “TopHat Alignment” app. Select version 1.0.0 on the top-right of the page, and click “Launch”.


  • Next to “Save Results To,” click “Select Project(s)”.


  • Select the “RNA-seq Methods Results” project you created in a previous step, and click “Confirm.”


  • Next to “Samples,” click “Select Sample(s).”


  • Select the four samples as before (“mRNA-Brain-C6,” “mRNA-Brain-C4,” “mRNA-UHRR-C2,” and “mRNA-UHRR-C1”), and click “Confirm.”


  • Click “Continue” to start running the app.


    • You must wait for TopHat to finish before starting Cufflinks. This process takes about ten hours to run.
